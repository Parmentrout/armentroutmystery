You've got questions, we've got answers.


How many puzzles will I receive?

You will receive 1 puzzle a week for 6 weeks delivered to your mailbox! Who doesn't love getting mail!? If you'd like to receive all of the puzzles at once simply select "Send All Together" at checkout.


Do I need anything to complete the puzzles?

Just an appetite for solving mysteries! And a pencil...


Am I going to get only Sudoku puzzles?

The puzzle type is different every week but the puzzles share a common theme. We're not saying there won't be a Sudoku puzzle though. Because there might be. Or not...


What if I hate puzzles?

Armentrout Mysteries escape rooms also make a great gift!
